About Us

Welcome to Mirissa Turtle Village Snorkeling & Diving Center, your perfect destination for unforgettable ocean adventures on the stunning south coast of Sri Lanka. We are dedicated to offering the best snorkeling and diving experiences, combining adventure, relaxation, and safety for every guest.

  • Safety

    Your safety is our number one priority. Our professional, certified dive instructors and guides strictly follow international safety standards to ensure you enjoy your adventure with confidence and peace of mind.

  • Modern & Reliable Equipment

    We provide high-quality, well-maintained snorkeling and diving gear to give you the most comfortable and secure experience. Whether you’re exploring vibrant reefs or swimming alongside turtles, our equipment ensures excellent performance in every dive.

  • Discover the Wonders of Mirissa

    Join us to experience the breathtaking underwater world of Mirissa. Swim with sea turtles, explore colorful coral reefs, and enjoy the beauty of the Indian Ocean. From first-time snorkelers to experienced divers, our tours are designed for all skill levels, guided by a friendly and knowledgeable team that makes every trip memorable.
